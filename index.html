Welcome!

My name is Philipp and I am a PhD student under the supervison of Jan Bruinier at the TU Darmstadt. I am interested in algebraic as well analytic number theory, complex geometry and automorphic forms. Currently I'm working a lot with Higher Green functions and therefore with Theta Lifts and weak Maass forms. I'm intereted in getting more familiar with Eisenstein series and the representation theoretic site of automorphic forms.
